# Ridgeline Finance — Copy & Information Architecture (v1)

Review doc for Chris + Emma. This is a draft of every piece of copy that will appear on the rebuilt site, plus the section-by-section structure of each page. Once approved, this becomes the source of truth for the build.

Read with a pen. Flag anything that feels off. Open items for Emma are called out at the bottom.

---

## Voice & tone

- **First-person plural.** We / our / us. No more switching to "I" on About.
- **Calm, disciplined, founder-first.** We are the steady hand. We are not salesy, not cutesy, not casually familiar.
- **Plain language over jargon.** Say "monthly close" not "monthly financial reporting cadence."
- **Short sentences.** Most under 20 words.
- **Em-dashes and italics used sparingly.** The current site leans on both as a stylistic tic; we cut ~70%.
- **Every sentence earns its spot.** If a line isn't helping the reader make a decision or build trust, it's out.

AP, AR, Payroll, Accounting, and Reporting systems will all be integrated to create a reliable, real-time understanding of the business.

Our sweet spot is $0–$50M in revenue. We scale the engagement to match your stage — from first finance hire through multi-entity complexity.

### 4. What makes us different

**Section headline:** Our approach.

Four differentiators. Each has a headline, a one-line subhead, and a supporting paragraph. Order matters — leading with the two-client cap makes every other claim credible.

**Two clients at a time. That's it.**
*Depth, not volume.*
Most fractional controllers juggle eight to ten clients at once. Ridgeline takes a maximum of two — which means your business gets real attention, fast response times, and a partner who actually knows your books.

**We run the stack, so you don't have to.**
*Finance systems end-to-end: evaluate, implement, optimize, maintain.*
You shouldn't need to become a QuickBooks admin or a NetSuite expert to run your business. The systems layer is ours to own — from selection through implementation through the monthly workflows that keep everything running quietly in the background.

**Flat fees. No scope creep. No surprise invoices.**
*You know what finance costs before we start.*
Traditional fractional engagements run on hourly billing, which means every conversation, every extra transaction, every month-end scramble becomes a line item. Ridgeline prices by outcome, not by hour — so the cost is predictable and the incentives are aligned with getting the work right.

**Licensed CPA. Senior-led. Audit-ready by default.**
*The person selling the engagement is the person doing the work.*
Most fractional services aren't staffed by licensed CPAs, and most regional firms hand off your books to junior staff once the contract is signed. At Ridgeline, you work directly with a CPA and MBA who has built and run finance functions from the inside — no handoffs, no rotating team, no learning on your books.
